Project Outcome

Prepare Terrains through JMARS

JMARS XR Viewer allows you to prepare 3D terrains through the Java Mission-planning and Analysis for Remote Sensing (JMARS) application, and view them in AR and VR.

JMARS, developed at the Mars Space Flight Facility (MSFF), is a planetary GIS that provides mission planning and data analysis tools to NASA scientists, instrument team members and the public.

View Planetary Terrains in AR

The JMARS AR mobile app allows users to scan QR codes or sign in with their JMARS credentials, to view any planetary terrain available in the JMARS application in Augmented Reality.

Terrains can be accessed by scanning QR codes prepared on the JMARS desktop application or signing in with their JMARS credentials to access saved data.

Future Plans

We envision JMARS XR Viewer to assist planetary scientists in communication and dissemination of their findings. We also see this a a good opportunity for public outreach, to increase support for space exploration.

About Me

A little about myself

I spend a lot of my time focusing on academics and working in my two jobs, but I love to keep busy and I make sure that my life is never filled with a dull moment. There are so many things that I want to learn and do, so I always find myself picking up new hobbies.

Over the last couple years, I have been teaching myself to play guitar, learning to cook strange and innovative dishes, and have recently become an enthusiastic plant Mom.

Another passion that I have always held close to my heart is art, and I feel so grateful to have been able to explore this passion more deeply during a semester abroad in Florence, Italy where I got to learn about the history and technical expertise that surrounds the city.

I am happiest when I am taking on new challenges. Even though I never initally saw myself working with technology, it is clear to me that technology is a ubiquitous part of Western culture and when combined with art can affect people in profound ways.

Over the last few years, I have found a love for UI design, and want to spend my life utilizing the intersection of arts, media, and engineering to spread social justice.

About

A little about myself

Hi, I'm Hannah, traveler, guitar hero enthusiast, and artist.

I am happiest when I am utilizing my creative outlet and bringing new artistic pieces to life. My heart is full when I am crafting anything from an innovative prototype to a transformed jacket. I see art as an important communication tool to spread ideas and instill change that would be otherwise impossible.

Growing up, I never saw myself having being interested in technology. It is now clear that technology is a ubiquitous part of our Western culture and when combined with art can have affect people in profound ways.

I want to spend my life utilizing the intersection of arts, media, and engineering to spread social justice.

Art

I have always been happiest when I am utilizing my creative outlet and bringing new aristic pieces to life. My heart is full when I am crafting anything from an innovative prototype to a transformed jacket.

While studying in Florence, I got the opportunity to learn artistic techniques from professionals and realized after being the top of my class of advanced art students, that I could actually follow this passion as a career path.

Returning to school at ASU, I was determined to find a way to implement my artistic passsion, and now skill, into my work as a designer.

Music

Music has always been an important part of my life. I live in a family of musicians and it has always allowed us to connect on a deeper level and grow from eachother's diverse persepectives on music.

The coolest thing about music, to me, is the profound effect that it can have on a person. A single song is able to drastically change a person's mood or perspective on a situaiton, in a way that simple words could never accomplish.

Because music has always been the easiest form of art for me to connect with, music showed me the power of art and it's affect on people. Music opened up a door for me to understand the impact of all art.

Travel

I have always been fascinated with learning different cultures and points of views. I think that traveling is the best opportunity to do so, and an important part of the human experience.

Living in Italy for four months has really opened my eyes to understanding how diverse people in this big world can be. I now understand that just because something seems like a fact to me, does not mean that it is true for everyone.

My favorite thing about Italian culture is that they work to live, where it seems like most people in Western culture live to work. In Italy people work as much as they need to have enough for basic human needs. I am a very dedicated worker and it would be hard for me to not be the best at what I do. But, I learned from this that if I am going to put so much into my work I need to be doing something that I truly love.
